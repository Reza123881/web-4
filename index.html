My name is Reza from Pondok Pakualam, Alam Sutera. Here, I will explain about my family. As the first child, I have to educate my two younger siblings to be better than me because being the first child is not as easy as people say. The first child must have a high level of understanding and strong ethics, along with a great sense of responsibility.

I have two younger sisters whom I must protect as their older sibling, because protecting them is the responsibility of an older sibling. It is my duty to ensure that nothing undesirable happens to them.
